FAQ

Common questions

Straight answers to things students usually ask.

How are lessons conducted?

Zoom with shared screens. I explain concepts step by step, then we practise questions together. You will always get a mix of guidance, practice, and exam strategies — not just "watch me solve."

How long is each lesson?

1.5 hours by default, but flexible. Consultation-style or longer 2-hour sessions (with a 5-min break) are both possible depending on your needs.

Can I use my school's materials?

Yes, definitely — for 1-to-1 students. We can work through your tutorials, lecture notes, or any other resources. I'll share extra practice and summaries when needed.

Is there a referral benefit?

Yes! For each friend you refer who continues with tuition, you get $25 cashback via PayNow/PayLah.

Can you guarantee an A?

I can guarantee you'll learn something — but grades take effort from both sides. Students who practise consistently alongside their lessons see the best results. I'll match your commitment and more.
